TRANSFORMING PROCESS INDUSTRIES

The Digital Process Manufacturing Centre (DPMC) is a specialist technology center by NMIS, focused on transforming the UK's process industries. We help businesses in sectors like pharmaceuticals, food and drink, and chemicals to improve productivity, ensure compliance, and reduce their carbon footprint through digital technologies.

Our goal is to accelerate the adoption of Industry 4.0, helping manufacturers embrace a digital future.

OUR CAPABILITIES

The DPMC provides a Digital Demonstration Laboratory for hands-on experience with Industry 4.0 technologies. We offer a collaborative space for industry partners and provide leadership training for digital strategy development. Our technical capabilities include automation, advanced computer-based tools, digital data management, and next-generation developments like 5G, AR, and VR.
